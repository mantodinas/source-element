The Invisible Reach

Through evaporation or boiling above 100°C, water transcends visible form. High kinetic energy propels molecules apart, creating steam a potent carrier of thermal energy.

Invisible Water Vapor

True water vapor is an odorless, colorless gas; the "steam" we see is actually condensing liquid droplets.
