Invisible Water Vapor: 
True water vapor is an odorless, colorless gas; the "steam" we see is actually condensing liquid droplets.
	https://courses.ems.psu.edu/learningweather/node/60

The Greenhouse Effect: 
Water vapor is the most abundant greenhouse gas, playing a critical role in Earth's temperature regulation.
	https://science.nasa.gov/earth/climate-change/steamy-relationships-how-atmospheric-water-vapor-amplifies-earths-greenhouse-effect/

Latent Heat: 
The process of evaporation stores energy in gas, which later fuels weather patterns when it condenses.
	https://science.gsfc.nasa.gov/earth/climate/researchareas/155
